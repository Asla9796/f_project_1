About Us



We are an IIT Madras students’ initiative to change the way you order and have food!

Specifically designed to make dining a unique experience, sQippr is your next stop at an all in one food ordering solution. Operating mostly out of IT Parks, colleges and food courts at malls, it provides the ultimate pathway to get rid of queues!

No more waiting at counters, order through sQippr, specify your time of delivery/take-away, be there and enjoy the meal you love!


Unlike other food ordering apps, our services don’t just stop at the customer end. Through customised online portals being made for each food-court, we increase the efficiency and provide a huge value add to their business.


Our prime features include

Pre-Ordering

Order your favourite food and choose a time of delivery of your choice! Do not let long, exasperating queues get in the of you and your food



Multi-Caterer services

Order from multiple caterers from a food court in a single cart and pay at once! Wait no more to perform multiple transactions if you want to relish in your Tako Sushi and Green Apple Mojito combination



Customised selections and ordering

Do you like it spicy? Mild? Warm? Ice-cold? A little more paprika? Thyme? Worry not! You can give special instructions to your chef and have your food your way



Dynamic Menu

Ever been in a situation when you were looking forward to have something which sounded delicious only for your attendant to let you know that that item is not available? We know that feel too! With our dynamic menu, you can always check the availability of your favourites dishes and never be disappointed
