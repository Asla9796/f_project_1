PRIVACY POLICIES


Your meal will be ready at the precise time set by you. The meal will be held at the counter for not more than 30 mins.
Any loss of order due to the time lapse from your end will not be accounted for.



RETURN, REFUND & CANCELLATION


After placing the order customer can cancel the order within 15mins if he/she wish to provided there is a difference of minimum 30 minutes between the time of cancellation and order delivery/pick-up time specified initially. Being a food item we do not entertain Return. In case of any loss of order due to lapse from the caterer end, the consumer shall be refunded in not more than 10 days through NEFT.
